Pomodoro Clock

Pomodoro Clock is a very good technique to be more productive while learning. First time I read about and see this technique on the Odin Project and since then I use it.
It have three sessions: 1) Pomodoro - 25 min of working or learning, 2) Short break - 5 min, 3) Long break - after 4 session of pomodoro give us one long break of 15 min.
